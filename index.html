About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Sushant Bagul.

I created this website to showcase my GeeksForGeeks articles. Choosing topics on the GeeksForGeeks Write Portal can be challenging, as similar content often already exists. However, I thoroughly research each chosen topic and frame my articles in my own words.

My unique contribution usually includes a video demonstration of a sample project related to the article’s content. My goal is to empower readers to work on their own ideas and grasp concepts deeply by referring to both articles and sample projects. Through this process, I’ve learned a lot myself, and I’m excited to continue expanding my knowledge.
